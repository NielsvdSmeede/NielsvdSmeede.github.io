Welcome to my website!

I am Niels and I study HBO-ICT at the HZ in Middelburg. I think this program fits me, because I really enjoy programming. This is because it gives me a challenge I want to overcome. I'm easily distracted, so if I think something is boring, I'll quit pretty fast. With this program, that's not the case.

I was introduced to ICT by a friend. He was really into it and showed me how it works a little bit. I was instantly intrigued. Because of him, I started studying HBO-ICT at the HU in Utrecht. Sadly, because of Covid, I didn't have enough CE to pass. I did learn a lot about programming there. After that, I went searching for other schools. I found the HZ, and it seemed like a good school. After comparing some schools, I chose this one. So far I really enjoy it and I hope to learn a lot more the coming four years.

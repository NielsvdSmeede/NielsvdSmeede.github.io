My study choice


Motivate your study choice

One of my friends was a programmer in his free time. He wanted to make a game for his PWS. I thought this was interesting, so I asked if we could work together. Together with another friend, we made the game. I enjoyed it and started studying ICT in Utrecht. Sadly, I failed the program because I didn't invest enough time in it. After that, I went looking for other schools where I could study ICT. I found schools in Zwolle, Tilburg and Middelburg, amongst others. The HZ in Middelburg was the school that seemed the most fun and suited me the best.


Study choice activities

I didn't do a lot of study choice activities. The last two years I did another ICT study en thought it was a lot of fun. I looked at the website of the different school that offer the ICT program and the HZ seemed the most fun.


After completing the study...

After the study I want to work, but I don't know where yet. I'm hoping to get to know the different sides of ICT during an internship, or at least at the end of the study.
I want to learn how to program complicated algorithms and how machine learning works. I also want to learn how to use different programs, like Unity, and different programming languages.

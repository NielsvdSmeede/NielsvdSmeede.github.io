Frequently Asked Questions:


1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? V

Put your schoolcard on the printer.

2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ? V

Put your schoolcard on the printer. Select "document inscannen". Add your email to the mailing list and select it. Press start. Your document will now be scanned and send to your mail.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop? V

Go to the web shop and select what you want to buy by clicking on the item. Select wich option you want and click on add to cart. Go to your shopping cart and press checkout. Follow the remaining instructions.

4. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID? V

If you are sick or show symptoms, you should follow these instructions.

5. How can you book a project space in one of the wings? V

Go to the reservation page. Select where and when you want to book a space. Add any specifications and confirm reservation.

6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? V

You can park your car at the Poelendaelesingel 8, Middelburg. You can only enter with your HZ-card. From here it's a 5 minute walk to the HZ.
